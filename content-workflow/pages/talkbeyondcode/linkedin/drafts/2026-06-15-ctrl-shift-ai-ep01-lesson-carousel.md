### Slide 3
A hackathon forced the first leap.
His team needed a Chrome extension, but nobody had browser-extension experience. ChatGPT helped them build one anyway.

### Slide 4
First, AI was a feature.
Prompt-based themes, LLM wrappers, and chatbot experiments made AI useful inside product surfaces.

### Slide 5
Then AI became infrastructure.
RAG systems, MCP, and dynamic UI moved the work from prompting a model to building systems around one.

Architecture first. Agents second.
